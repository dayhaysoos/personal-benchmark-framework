# Content And Licensing

This is practical open-source hygiene, not legal advice.

## Framework License

The recommended framework license is MIT.

## Downstream Licenses

Downstream benchmark authors should choose a license for their own benchmark content.

Common options:

- MIT or Apache-2.0 for permissive reuse
- CC BY 4.0 for attribution-focused written content
- CC BY-NC 4.0 for noncommercial sharing
- All rights reserved for public viewing with limited reuse

## Content Guidance

- Use your own descriptions and judgments.
- Do not copy large copyrighted source material into scenarios.
- Quote sparingly and cite when needed.
- Avoid publishing private or proprietary data unless you own it or have permission.
- Avoid implying official affiliation with games, products, companies, or communities unless you have permission.

Downstream benchmark repos may use brand-adjacent names. Be clear about the domain being evaluated without implying endorsement or official status.
